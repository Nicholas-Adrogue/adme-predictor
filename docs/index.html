What is SMILES?

SMILES is a text notation for representing molecular structures. For example, "O" is water, "CC" is ethane, and "c1ccccc1" is benzene.

What is logD?

logD measures lipophilicity — how well a drug dissolves in fats vs water at physiological pH. It affects absorption, distribution, and metabolism.

Lipinski's Rules

The "Rule of Five" predicts oral drug bioavailability based on molecular weight, logP, hydrogen bond donors, and acceptors.
